Chicken Dumplings Recipe

Description:

An easy crockpot chicken and dumplings recipe for a filling, comforting meal on a cold, snowy day. I have four children who are picky eaters, and they love this!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Canned Soup
  • Onion
  • Refrigerated Biscuit Dough

Steps:

  1. Basically, you'll just add the chicken, butter, soup, and onion to a slow cooker
  2. cook on high for about five or six hours
  3. Add the shredded biscuit dough about 30 minutes before serving. Let the dough cook in the soup until it's no longer raw
