Tortilla Soup Recipe

Description:

This easy chicken tortilla soup is a delicious, zesty soup recipe with only 6 canned ingredients! Serve topped with tortilla chips and shredded Cheddar cheese. Throw away the cans, and no one will know it is not from scratch!

Ingredients:

  • 2(14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1(15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1(15 ounce) can black beans
  • 1(10 ounce) can chunk chicken
  • 1(10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chili peppers, drained
  • crushed tortilla chips Optional
  • shredded Cheddar cheese Optional

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Pour chicken broth, corn, black beans, chunk chicken, and diced tomatoes with green chilies into a large saucepan or stockpot over medium heat
  3. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until chicken is heated through, about 15 minutes.
  4. Serve hot, topped with tortilla chips and grated cheese.
